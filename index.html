Welcome to Hui Hong's Website

I am Hui Hong (洪慧), a master student at Department of Astronomy, University of Science and Technology of China. I also completed my undergraduate studies at University of Science and Technology of China.


My research interests lie in astronomy and astrophysics, especially in galaxy formation and evolution. I use observational data to do statistical analysis aimed at unraveling the mysteries of galaxies. Currently I am working with MaNGA IFS data to study spatially resolved properties of nearby galaxies. I hope to study how galaxies evolve across the cosmic time.


I enjoy discussions and interactions on a wide range of astrophysical topics with my peers. These exchanges of ideas have been a source of inspiration for me. I am the organizer of Galaxy and Cosmology Seminar.
